Maintenance Plan Agreement

This Maintenance Plan Agreement outlines the terms, expectations, and conditions under which recurring maintenance services are provided by Star Light Mobile Detailing. These plans are designed to maintain an already detailed vehicle, not to correct neglect or heavy buildup.

Eligibility & Enrollment

  • An initial full detail is required before enrollment in any maintenance plan.
  • The vehicle must be in maintained condition at the time of enrollment.
  • Maintenance plans are vehicle-specific and non-transferable.
  • Enrollment confirms acceptance of all terms outlined in this agreement.

What Maintenance Covers

Exterior Maintenance
  • Hand wash and dry
  • Wheel and tire cleaning
  • Tire dressing
  • Exterior glass cleaning
  • Light visual inspection of paint condition
Interior Maintenance
  • Light vacuuming
  • Wipe-down of high-touch surfaces
  • Interior glass cleaning
  • Light dust and debris removal

What Is Not Included

  • Pet hair removal
  • Stain removal
  • Heavy interior cleaning
  • Carpet shampooing or steam cleaning
  • Paint decontamination or correction
  • Excessive sand, mud, or debris buildup

These services may be added at additional cost if needed.

Condition Standards

  • Maintenance pricing assumes the vehicle remains in maintained condition.
  • If excessive dirt, neglect, pet hair, stains, or buildup are present, add-ons may be required.
  • If condition exceeds maintenance scope, a reset detail may be recommended.
  • Final determination of condition is made on-site at the time of service.

Rollover & Missed Visits

  • One rollover is permitted per calendar year.
  • Rollover allows one missed visit to be applied to the next scheduled cycle.
  • Additional missed visits may void maintenance eligibility.
  • Extended gaps may require a reset full detail.

Scheduling & Weather

  • Service windows are scheduled based on availability.
  • Weather-related delays may occur due to rain, lightning, or unsafe conditions.
  • Rescheduling due to weather will be coordinated at no penalty.
  • Customer must provide reasonable access to the vehicle at the scheduled time.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Remove personal belongings prior to service.
  • Ensure vehicle is accessible and unlocked.
  • Disclose any known issues or sensitive areas.
  • Maintain reasonable cleanliness between visits.

Payment & Plan Changes

  • Payment is due at the time of each visit unless otherwise arranged.
  • Plans may be adjusted or canceled at any time.
  • Pricing is subject to adjustment if vehicle condition changes.
  • Failure to maintain condition may result in plan suspension.

Agreement Acknowledgment

Enrollment in a maintenance plan confirms understanding and acceptance of the terms outlined above. These plans are designed for clients who value consistent care and realistic expectations.
